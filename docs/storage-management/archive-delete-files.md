# Archive and Delete Files

The more your content grows over time, the harder it becomes to manage large amounts of data in your environment. Organizations sometimes need to retain older files for compliance, but keeping inactive data in active storage increases costs and makes it harder to manage your active content. 

Syskit Point helps you address this challenge by enabling administrators to archive or delete inactive SharePoint files directly from the Storage Metrics report.
